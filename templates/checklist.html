New patient / booking checklist

Schedule reference

Scope (procedure) booking days:

  • East Sydney Private Hospital — Monday
  • Mater Hospital — Wednesday
  • Dee Why Endoscopy — Friday

Consult clinic days:

  • Mater Clinic — Tuesday & Thursday mornings, alternate weeks
  • Northern Beaches Hospital Clinic — Thursday afternoon, alternate weeks
A. Patient booked by the AI phone system — needs confirming
  1. Ring the patient to confirm their appointment.
  2. Get their email address.
  3. Tell them a confirmation email is on its way.
  4. Log into Halaxy and enter the patient's email address there.
  5. Send them a welcome email via Callback Manager, with the correct room info attached — NBH Consultation Room Info or Mater Clinic Room Info, whichever clinic they're booked into (under "Other documents" on the email screen).
B. Patient wants to book a scope directly
  1. Tell them the scope days (see schedule above) and confirm which hospital suits them.
  2. Get their Medicare number.
  3. Get their private health insurance details — confirm they actually have hospital cover.
  4. Email Dr Tu the patient's details.
C. Sending scope booking documents
  • Colonoscopy only → attach Colonoscopy Prep.
  • Colonoscopy + gastroscopy → attach Colonoscopy Prep + Gastroscopy Prep.
  • Gastroscopy only → attach Gastroscopy Prep ONLY — not colonoscopy prep.
  • If they need cost clarified → attach the IFC.

Double-check the correct hospital is ticked before sending — documents are attached per hospital, not shared across them.

D. Patient asks about cost
  • Initial consult: $320
  • Follow-up consult: $150
  • Endoscopy: no gap or bulk billed, depending on their insurance status.
  • Policy excess may apply; a hospital fee applies if they don't have insurance.

Forward them the IFC and ask them to read it.
