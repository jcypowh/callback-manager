Users

Everyone shares one login password (set/changed under Settings). This controls who shows up in the "who are you" picker, their role, and their pay — editable by you or Sally. Hourly rate is what they're paid, worked out from the minutes they log against each task (e.g. 13 min at $30/hr = $6.50) — covers every logged attempt, completion, and hand-back, not just successes. Leave blank for no payment (e.g. Sally and Dr Tu aren't paid per task). Phone is where urgent SMS notifications go. Is doctor controls who "Forward to Dr Tu" actually goes to — only one person can be it at a time.
